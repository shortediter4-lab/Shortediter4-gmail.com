📚 What is StudyCoin?

StudyCoin is an innovative platform where students can learn new skills, complete educational tasks, and earn rewards for their progress. This project is currently in development to bridge the gap between study and motivation.

🎯 Our Goals

✅ Gamified Learning: Making study feel like a reward.

✅ Consistency: Rewarding students who show up every day.

✅ Community: Building a global network of learners.
